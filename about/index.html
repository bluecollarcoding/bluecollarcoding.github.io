Ramsays Renos

With a knack for building things since an early age, I recently returned to my first true passion of carpentry. This site serves as an archive of all my projects. Spending my 20s in academia then tech, the thing that always made me the most happy was working on fun DIY projects. In the spring/summer of 2019, we built a beautiful 200ft fence around the property I manage … and by August I was ready to take my construction skills to the next level. So, I shed the golden handcuffs and took a job as a helper building decks.

It taught me a trememdous amount and of course covid came along. The timing was right to take the leap and be my own boss. I put out a few Kijiji ads and the result has exceeded all my expectations. Enjoy!
